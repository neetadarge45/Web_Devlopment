About Us

At Coca-Cola, we are committed to refreshing the world and making a difference. Since our founding in 1886, we have been driven by a passion for innovation and a dedication to our consumers. With a rich history and a legacy of quality, our beverages have become an integral part of celebrations, everyday moments, and everything in between.

Our mission is to inspire moments of optimism and happiness through our brands and actions. We believe in the power of connection and the importance of sustainability. By investing in communities and the environment, we aim to create a better future for generations to come.

Our History

Coca-Cola was invented in 1886 by Dr. John Stith Pemberton. The first Coca-Cola was served at Jacobs' Pharmacy in Atlanta, Georgia. Since then, Coca-Cola has grown into one of the most recognizable brands globally, known for its refreshing taste and iconic advertising campaigns.

Our Mission

At Coca-Cola, our mission is to refresh the world in body and spirit, to inspire moments of optimism and happiness, and to create value and make a difference. We are committed to producing high-quality beverages while fostering a positive impact on the communities we serve.
